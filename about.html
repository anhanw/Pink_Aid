About us

About Us & The reason behind

Pink-Aid is a program developed by a team of four computer science students. The program focuses on raising awareness about women's health issues such as HPV, breast cancer, and menstrual disorders. The aim of Pink-Aid is to encourage women to pay more attention to their health and take proactive steps to prevent and detect diseases.

In addition to providing information, Pink-Aid also includes a forum feature that allows users to communicate with each other about their health. This feature provides a safe and supportive environment where women can share their experiences, ask questions, and receive advice from other user

Overall, Pink-Aid is an essential program that promotes women's health and gender equality. By providing accurate information, resources, and a supportive community, Pink-Aid empowers women to take control of their health and live healthier, happier lives.

Our Team

The team is committed to promoting gender equality by giving women the tools to make informed decisions about their bodies. With their expertise in programming, data analysis, and web development, they collaborate with health organizations to ensure credibility.
